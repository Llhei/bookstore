To log in, add the following to your tomcat-users.xml file:
<role rolename="admin"/>
<role rolename="sprole"/>
<user password="pwd" roles="admin" username="admin"/>
<user password="pwd" roles="sprole" username="user"/>

Then use the above details to log in.

Note: you will have to restart your tomcat server.


Current security configurations:
admin has access to all files.
sprole does not have access to following files:
- addbooks
- refund
